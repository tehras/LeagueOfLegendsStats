Welcome To LoL Stats!

This page is a brief description of this project. This app aims to target League of Legends players who want to check their statistics, current games, and be able to manage and see their favorite summoners as well as the top challengers.
Below you will see examples of how this app works!

Keep track of your summoner or add your favorite players and keep track of them!

Quick Search from any screen to find the summoner you're looking for

See overall statistics!

See in detail Ranked Solo Queue stats, like current standing, Recent match history, KDA, and more!

In detail game stats to see how you and your team has done!

See who you are matched up against even before the game loads up!
